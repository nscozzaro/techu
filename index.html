How to Play

Daily vs Practice: Daily uses one shared worldwide UTC shuffle each day. Practice starts a fresh random deck against the Bot.

Goal: Finish with more spaces than the Bot when both players are out of cards.

Core Rules

  • Higher cards outrank lower cards. Aces are highest.
  • Every card you play must connect back to your own home row.
  • Connections count only vertically and horizontally, never diagonally.
  • You may play on your own card or the Bot's card if your rank is higher.
  • Same-rank cards cannot be played on each other.

Start

  • Your home row depends on the color you choose: Red uses the bottom row and Black uses the top row.
  • Each side starts with 3 cards in hand.
  • Both players place a card on the center space of their home row.
  • The lower revealed card takes the first turn.
  • If the opening cards tie, both players draw again and repeat the opening play.

On Your Turn

  • Draw back up to 3 cards.
  • The first move of play must go on the board. After that, you may play 1 legal card or discard 1 card.
  • Once a placed card is revealed, that turn is locked in.

Controls

  • Drag a card from your hand to a legal space, or to Discard.
  • Click your face-down card on the board to reveal it.
  • Use `+` for game options, `?` for rules, and `⚙` for settings.

Techu: A Techu is a winning finish with a 3 on an opponent's 2, or a 2 on an empty space.
